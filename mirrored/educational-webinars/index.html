Educational Webinars

Athlete & Parent Educational Webinars

At SwimStrong Dryland, we’re dedicated to educating and empowering athletes, parents, and teams with the knowledge that fuels performance.

Each month (excluding December, March, July, and August), an SSDL staff member or guest expert hosts a live Zoom webinar on topics like training, recovery, nutrition, leadership, and mindset. Webinars are open to both athletes and parents, making them a powerful resource for the whole community.

Find links to upcoming sessions and recordings of past webinars below. Questions? Reach out by email (individual athletes) or through our Contact Us page (club teams).
